### Inscription

>In memory of <br>
>Joshua Jeays <br>
>Late Mayor of Brisbane <br>
>Who died 11^th^ March, 1881 <br>
>Aged 69 <br>
>and of <br>
>Sarah his wife <br>
>Who died 26^th^ July, 1861 <br>
>Aged 52 <br>
>She is buried at Milton <br>
>Be just and fear not <br>
