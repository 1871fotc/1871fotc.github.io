# Elizabeth Dale

**1865 — 1 February 1905**

--8<-- "snippets/elizabeth-dale.md"

### Headstone

![Elizabeth Dale](../assets/elizabeth-dale-headstone.jpg){ width="32%" }

### Inscription

> Elizabeth <br>
> beloved wife of <br>
> Thomas Dale <br>
> died Feb. 1^st^ 1905 <br>
> aged 40 years <br>
> Thy will be done <br>
