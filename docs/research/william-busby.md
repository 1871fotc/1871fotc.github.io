# William Busby

**ca. May 1857 - 14 April 1928**

### Inscription

>In loving rememberance <br>
>of <br>
>William Busby <br>
>Died 4^th^ April 1928, <br>
>Aged 76 years 11 months. <br>
>Also his wife  <br>
>Annie <br>
>Died 10^th^ May 1932, <br>
>Aged 84 years. <br>
>At rest.
