# Kevin Izod O'Doherty 

**1823 — 1905**

### Headstone

![Kevin Izod O'Doherty](../assets/kevin-izod-odoherty-headstone.jpg){ width="30%" }

### Inscription

>
Sacred <br>
to the memory of <br>
Kevin Izod O'Doherty, <br>
The Irish Patroit. <br>
Died 15^th^ July 1905, Aged 81 Years <br>
Whose name will live in Irish history <br>
and whise memory ever remains en- <br>
shrined in Irish hearts at home and <br>
abroad. <br>
 <br>
Also his gifted wiife <br>
"Eva of the Nation" <br>
Died 22^nd^ May 1910, Aged 80 Years <br>
 <br>
Requiescant in pace <br>
 <br>
This monument is erected bu admirers <br>
of the late Doctor O'Doherty and his wife, <br>
as a mark of appreciation of their un- <br>
sullied patriotism and exalted devotion <br>
to the cause of Irish freedom. <br>
>
